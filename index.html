Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using Venmo.

How do I reset my Venmo password?

To reset your password, go to the Venmo login page and click "Forgot password?" Enter the email or phone number associated with your account, and you'll receive instructions to reset your password.

Why is my payment pending?

Payments may be pending for several reasons: the recipient hasn't accepted the payment, there's an issue with your payment method, or Venmo's security team is reviewing the transaction. Most pending payments complete within 1-3 business days.

How do I add a bank account to Venmo?

To add a bank account: 1) Tap the "☰" menu, 2) Select "Settings", 3) Tap "Payment Methods", 4) Select "Add bank or card", 5) Choose "Bank" and follow the instructions to connect your account.

Is Venmo safe to use?

Venmo uses bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your information. They also offer purchase protection for eligible transactions and recommend enabling multi-factor authentication for added security.
